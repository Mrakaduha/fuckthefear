Fuck the Fear

A small project about fear, breath, and the space between.

Throughout life, you never stay the same even in two consecutive days. Your body replaces itself in countless ways, and only two things stay with you from birth to death: your fear and your breath. And so long as you remain in the current of life, the current decides for you. If you want to choose for yourself you must look for the gaps — for only in a gap can you set a direction of your own, before the current claims you again.

About the book

Fuck the Fear explores fear as a natural companion. The book offers practices, short essays and simple designs for daily attention. Minimalist layout, practical items, and an invitation to write your own book.

Gaps

Set the chart perpendicular to your face and look at it with crossed eyes, i.e. the right eye rest on the left column and the left one on the right column. Distance: about 80 cm. This way of looking creates a cognitive dissonance in the visual center of the brain: Is it red? Or is it blue? The gap ensues from this.
