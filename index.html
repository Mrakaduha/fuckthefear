Fuck the Fear

A small project about fear, breath, and the space between.

Throughout life, you never stay the same even two consecutive days. Your body replaces itself in countless ways, only two things stay with you from birth to death: your fear and your breath. And so long as you remain in the current of life, the current decides for you. If you want to choose for yourself you must look for gaps — for only in a gap can you set a direction of your own, before the current claims you again.

About the book

The book is a distillery. The author refines moments gathered over a lifetime into small, tangible drops, simple enough to carry, strong enough to return to when needed. It does not offer a finished potion. Instead, it suggests a way of working: observing, distilling, and creating something of your own. With some luck, you may also find a few hints, and a few ingredients, along the way.
